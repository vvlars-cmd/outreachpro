How it works

From zero leads to
booked meetings in 4 steps

01
Scrape leads from Google Maps
Enter target cities and search terms. OutreachPro uses Apify to find local businesses and extract contact details automatically — name, email, phone, website, rating, and address.
⚡ Apify · compass/crawler-google-places
02
AI personalises every email
Claude reads each lead's business data and writes a unique personalised opening line. Click "Personalise All" and every email in your campaign gets a tailored hook — in seconds.
✦ claude-sonnet-4 · Per-lead context
03
Launch campaign sequences
Build multi-step sequences with day-based delays. Set your daily send limit, enable stop-on-reply, and launch. OutreachPro handles the rest — rate limiting, retries, and tracking.
📧 Gmail OAuth · Warmup-safe sending
04
Manage replies and close deals
Replies land in Unibox and are auto-labelled by AI. Move interested leads to the CRM, track them through your pipeline, and measure revenue from first contact to signed deal.

FAQ

Common questions

Is my data safe? Who can see my leads? ↓
OutreachPro is fully self-hosted. Your leads, campaigns, and email content never leave your server. We don't have access to any of your data. You bring your own API keys for Anthropic, Apify, and Google — all requests go directly from your server to those services.
What are the ongoing API costs? ↓
The OutreachPro licence fee covers the software. You'll also need: Anthropic API (~€0.01–0.05 per personalised email), Apify (~€5–15 per 1,000 leads scraped), and a Google Cloud project (free tier usually sufficient). Most Pro users spend €20–80/month on API costs depending on volume.
How do I install it? Do I need a developer? ↓
If you can install Docker Desktop, you can run OutreachPro. It's one command: docker compose up --build. The full setup (including API keys) takes under 20 minutes. We provide step-by-step documentation for non-technical users.
Is cold emailing legal in Europe / GDPR? ↓
B2B cold email is legal in most EU countries when you email business addresses found via legitimate sources (like Google Maps) and have a legitimate interest basis. OutreachPro automatically includes unsubscribe links in all emails. We recommend consulting your legal counsel for country-specific guidance. The tool is designed to comply with GDPR legitimate interest provisions for B2B outreach.
Can I try it before paying? ↓
Yes — the full source code is on GitHub (MIT licence, valid 6 months). You can run it locally for free to evaluate it. The paid plans add licence rights for commercial use, priority support, and access to future updates and new features.
What's the difference between the plans? ↓
Starter is capped at 500 leads and 1 user — great for testing. Pro removes all caps, adds the CRM, analytics, Gmail scraper, and multi-account warmup — the most popular choice for active sales teams. Agency adds multi-workspace client isolation, white-labelling, and WARP AI mode for maximum automation. Enterprise is fully custom.
