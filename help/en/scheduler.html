Meeting Scheduler — Built-in Calendly Replacement

Give every lead a booking link. They pick a slot from your availability, and OutreachPro automatically creates a CRM deal, sends a confirmation, and logs the meeting. No Calendly subscription needed — €0/month vs Calendly's €16/month.

Setting Up Your Scheduler

  1. Go to Settings → Meeting Scheduler
  2. Set your meeting name (e.g. "30-Min CycleWASH Demo")
  3. Set duration (15, 30, or 60 minutes)
  4. Set your timezone
  5. Configure available time slots per day
  6. Customise the confirmation message template
  7. Click Save

Adding Your Booking Link to Emails

Include your booking URL in any email template:

Hi {{name}},

I'd love to show you the CycleWASH Pro in action.
Pick any slot that works for you:

👉 yoursite.com/scheduler

It's a quick 30-minute demo — I'll show you
exactly how it works in a shop like {{name}}'s.

Best,
[Your name]

What Happens When a Lead Books

  1. Lead visits /scheduler and picks a time slot
  2. Slot is instantly marked as booked (no double-booking possible)
  3. CRM deal is automatically created in "Meeting" stage with the lead's details
  4. Confirmation message is displayed on screen
  5. You see the new deal in your CRM Pipeline immediately

Booking Link in Sequences

Add the booking URL as the CTA in any sequence step. Pair it with a Smart Sequence condition: if a lead clicks the booking link, immediately advance them to "Meeting" stage in CRM and skip remaining email steps.

Availability Configuration Example

Mon–Thu: 09:00, 10:00, 11:00, 14:00, 15:00, 16:00

Weekend: (no slots)

API

GET  /api/scheduler/config    → current availability settings
POST /api/scheduler/config    → update availability
GET  /api/scheduler/available → next 14 days of open slots
POST /api/scheduler/book      → book a slot {slotId, name, email, company}
GET  /scheduler               → public booking page (share this URL)
