One-Click Proposal Generator

When a CRM deal reaches "Proposal" stage, generate a complete, print-ready sales proposal in one click. Claude writes a personalised executive summary. OutreachPro fills in the product details, ROI calculations, and pricing. The result is a professional branded proposal you can email, share as a link, or print — without touching a design tool. PandaDoc charges €35/month for this. It's built in.

Generating a Proposal

  1. Open a CRM deal in "Proposal" stage
  2. Click Generate Proposal
  3. Optionally add a custom message for Claude to incorporate
  4. Choose products to include and apply a discount if relevant
  5. Click Generate — proposal renders in ~5 seconds
  6. Preview the HTML proposal, then click Send via Gmail or Copy Link

What the Proposal Includes

  • Cover page — client name, city, date, reference number, validity period
  • Executive summary — 2 sentences written by Claude, personalised to the specific business (references their rating, category, city)
  • Product section — your products with pricing, featured recommendation highlighted
  • ROI table — cleaning time reduction, throughput increase, payback period
  • CTA section — bold call-to-action with your booking link
  • Footer — company details, address, registration number

Example Executive Summary (Claude-written)

"With 342 Google reviews and a 4.8-star rating, Fahrrad Müller has clearly built Köln's most trusted bike shop. CycleWASH Pro is designed for exactly this type of high-volume premium operation — cutting per-bike cleaning time from 15 minutes to under 4, letting your team serve more customers while maintaining the quality your reputation is built on."

Customising Products and Pricing

The proposal generator uses your workspace product settings by default. Override for any specific deal:

  • Add/remove products from the proposal
  • Apply a percentage discount (shown as a discount badge)
  • Add custom line items (installation, training, extended warranty)

Validity Period

Every proposal automatically includes a 30-day validity date. This creates urgency without a hard sell — the lead knows the pricing is only locked in for 30 days. You can adjust the validity period in Settings → Proposal Defaults.

Sharing Options

  • Email HTML — copy the full HTML into a Gmail email (renders beautifully in most email clients)
  • Direct link — share the proposal URL directly: the lead can view it in any browser
  • Print/PDF — open in browser → print → save as PDF for physical meetings

API

POST /api/proposals/generate
  Body: { dealId, leadId, customMessage, products[], discount }
  → { proposal, html, previewUrl }

GET /api/proposals → list all generated proposals
