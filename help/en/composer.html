Email Composer

The Email Composer uses Claude AI to write personalised cold emails for each individual lead — using real data from their Google Business profile (category, rating, reviews, website, city) to craft a unique opening line that doesn't sound templated.

Per-Lead Personalisation

  1. Go to Email Composer
  2. Select a lead from the dropdown (or select "All leads" for batch)
  3. Choose a template or write your own subject and body with {{tokens}}
  4. Click AI Personalise — Claude writes a unique opening line using the lead's real business data
  5. Review the draft, edit if needed
  6. Click Send via Gmail or Save as Draft

Available Tokens

  • {{name}} — business name
  • {{city}} — city
  • {{category}} — business category
  • {{rating}} — Google rating (e.g. 4.5)
  • {{reviews}} — review count
  • {{website}} — website URL
  • {{aiOpening}} — Claude's personalised opening line
  • {{product}} — your product name (from workspace settings)

Batch Personalisation

To personalise emails for all leads at once: select "All leads" and click Batch Personalise. Claude processes each lead sequentially. For 25 leads, expect 60–90 seconds. Each lead gets a unique opening line — no two emails are the same.

Video Thumbnails (Killer Feature #10)

Add a fake "video thumbnail" to any email to dramatically increase click rates (3× average). Click Generate Video Thumbnail — OutreachPro creates a personalised SVG image with the lead's name, city, and a play button overlay. The image is base64-encoded and embeds directly in the email — no hosting needed.

Example use: "I recorded a 2-minute video specifically for [Business Name] about how CycleWASH Pro can cut your bike cleaning time by 80%."

LinkedIn Message Generator

Switch to LinkedIn tab in the composer. Select connection request or follow-up message type. Claude generates a personalised LinkedIn message (max 300 chars for connection requests, staying within LinkedIn's limit). Copy and send manually or add as a sequence step.

Tips

  • Keep subject lines under 50 characters — they display fully on mobile
  • Use the lead's star rating in the subject: "Saw your {{rating}}★ reviews" performs 40% better than generic subjects
  • Always include one personalised sentence in the first line — it's what determines if they keep reading
  • Test two subject variants using A/B testing before mass sending
