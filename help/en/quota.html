Quota & Commission Tracker

Set your monthly revenue target and OutreachPro tracks progress in real time — showing you how much you've won, what commission you've earned, and whether you're on pace to hit quota. Salesforce SPM charges $350+/user/month for this. Free in OutreachPro.

Setting Up

  1. Go to Analytics → Quota & Commission
  2. Set monthly revenue target (e.g. €50,000)
  3. Set base commission rate (e.g. 5% of won deal value)
  4. Set bonus threshold — e.g. 100% of quota = bonus kicks in
  5. Set bonus commission rate (e.g. 10% on revenue above target)
  6. Save

What Gets Tracked

  • Won this month — sum of all deals marked Won since month start
  • % of quota — won value ÷ monthly target × 100
  • On track? — compares current % to how far through the month you are
  • Base commission — won value × commission rate
  • Bonus commission — activates when you hit quota threshold
  • Days left — remaining selling days this month

Example

Monthly quota: €50,000
Commission: 5% base, 10% bonus above quota
Day 15 of 30 (50% through month)

Won so far: €28,000 (56% of quota)
On track: ✓ (56% > 50% day target)
Commission earned: €1,400 base + €0 bonus
Projected month-end: €56,000 → bonus kicks in

API

GET  /api/quota   → current progress + commission calculation
POST /api/quota   → update {monthly, commissionRate, bonusThreshold, bonusPct}
