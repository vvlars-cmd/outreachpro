Daily Task Queue — Sales Workspace

Salesforce's Spring '26 headline feature is "Sales Workspace" — a command centre showing every rep exactly what to do today. OutreachPro's Daily Task Queue does the same thing: one prioritised list combining follow-up reminders, hot signals, stale deals, sequence steps due, and A/B tests to review. Claude writes a personalised daily briefing at the top.

What Appears in the Queue

  • Priority 1 — Follow-ups due (high urgency) — Memory CRM reminders that are due today or overdue. A lead said "call me in May" and it's May — they're at the top.
  • Priority 2 — Hot buying signals (high urgency) — leads who clicked a link or opened an email recently. Strike while it's hot.
  • Priority 3 — Stale deals (medium urgency) — CRM deals with no activity in 7–30 days. These are quietly dying without attention.
  • Priority 4 — Sequence steps due (medium urgency) — Smart Sequence next steps that are ready to send today.
  • Priority 5 — A/B test results (low urgency) — completed tests waiting for review and winner confirmation.

Daily Briefing

Claude reads your task list and writes a 1-sentence action-oriented briefing: "You have 3 overdue follow-ups from warm leads and 2 hot signals from this week — focus on the follow-ups first, then the clicked-email leads before end of day."

Using the Queue

  1. Open OutreachPro every morning → Dashboard → Today's Tasks
  2. Start with Priority 1 (follow-ups) — these are leads who gave you a specific date
  3. Work through hot signals (Priority 2) — call or email within 2 hours of a click
  4. Check stale deals (Priority 3) — one touch each, quick
  5. Send sequence steps (Priority 4) — review and fire

API

GET /api/tasks/today
→ {
    taskCount, highUrgency,
