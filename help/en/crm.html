CRM Pipeline

The CRM gives you a Kanban view across six stages: Prospect → Contacted → Meeting → Proposal → Won → Lost.

Adding Deals

Click + Add Deal. Enter company name, deal value (€), and initial stage. You can also click the + Add button at the bottom of any column.

Moving Deals

Click a deal card and select a new stage, or drag between columns. Deals move through: Prospect (identified) → Contacted (emailed) → Meeting (call booked) → Proposal (quote sent) → Won/Lost.

CycleWASH Deal Values

  • Pro Platinum: €38,000 net per unit
  • Mini Platinum: €27,500 net per unit

Integration with Unibox

When a lead replies and you label them "Interested" in Unibox, they're automatically created as a deal in the Contacted stage. "Meeting booked" creates a Meeting-stage deal.
