Account Research

Before calling or emailing a lead, you should know: who the decision maker likely is, what their pain points are, what buying signals their Google profile shows, and how hot they are. OutreachPro's Account Research uses Claude to generate a strategic brief for any lead in one click. Salesforce calls this "Relationship Insights" and charges for it in their Unlimited tier at $350+/user/month.

What the Brief Includes

  • Decision maker title — likely title of the person who signs off on purchases (e.g. "Inhaber", "Geschäftsführer")
  • Business size — inferred from review count and category (solo, small, medium, large)
  • Buying signals — positive indicators from their profile (e.g. "Excellent reputation suggests premium positioning", "High review growth = actively investing in business")
  • Pain points — likely problems your product solves for this type of business
  • Talking points — specific angles for your opening line, tailored to this business
  • Competitor risk — how likely they already have a competing solution (low/medium/high)
  • Overall rating — cold / warm / hot

Generating a Brief

  1. Open any lead in Lead Finder
  2. Click Account Research
  3. Claude analyses their Google Business profile data (name, city, category, rating, reviews, website) and returns the brief in ~3 seconds
  4. Brief is cached on the lead — no repeated API calls
