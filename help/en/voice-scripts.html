Voice Message Script Generator

Claude writes personalised voicemail and WhatsApp voice message scripts for each lead. The script is tailored to their specific business — using their name, city, category, star rating, and review count to make the message feel researched, not templated. Includes [PAUSE] and [EMPHASISE] markers for natural, confident delivery.

Why Voice Still Works in 2026

HubSpot's 2026 State of Sales report found that 42% of B2B prospects respond better to social and voice outreach than email. A 30-second voicemail after 2 unanswered emails can unlock leads who've been ignoring your emails — the channel switch alone signals persistence and professionalism.

Generating a Script

  1. Go to Email Composer → Voice Scripts
  2. Select a lead from the dropdown
  3. Choose duration: 30 sec (connection request) or 60 sec (full pitch)
  4. Choose tone: friendly, professional, direct, or warm
  5. Choose language: de, en, fr, es, etc.
  6. Click Generate Script
  7. Review and edit — then record via your phone or WhatsApp

Best Practices for Voice Outreach

  • Call timing — Tuesday–Thursday, 10–11am and 2–4pm have the highest answer rates
  • Leave a voicemail — if no answer, always leave the script. 27% of voicemails generate a callback
  • WhatsApp voice — in Germany, WhatsApp voice messages have an 85%+ listen rate. Higher than email open rates
  • Reference your email — always mention you sent an email, so they can find it if interested
  • Maximum 30 seconds — anything longer gets deleted without finishing

The 3-Touch Sequence with Voice

Day 0  — Email: personalised pitch
Day 3  — Voicemail: 30-second personal script
Day 5  — WhatsApp: same script as voice note
Day 7  — Email: follow-up referencing "I left you a voicemail"
Day 14 — LinkedIn: connection request
