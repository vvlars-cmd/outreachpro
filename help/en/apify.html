Apify Lead Scraper

OutreachPro integrates directly with Apify's Google Maps scraper to pull real business data — name, email, phone, website, rating, reviews, address, category — into your lead database in minutes.

Quick Start

  1. Create a free account at apify.com
  2. Copy your API token from Account → Integrations
  3. Add it to your .env file as APIFY_TOKEN=apify_api_...
  4. In OutreachPro: go to Apify Scraper, enter a search term (e.g. "Fahrradhändler") and a city (e.g. "Köln")
  5. Click Run Scrape — results stream in live via SSE

Multi-City Bulk Scraper

The bulk scraper (Killer Feature #6) queues multiple cities in one run. Go to Apify Scraper → Bulk Mode, enter a comma-separated list of cities and search terms. The scraper runs each combination and auto-imports leads that score above your threshold.

Cities: Köln, Düsseldorf, Bonn, Aachen, Münster
Terms: Fahrradhändler, E-Bike Händler, Fahrrad Werkstatt
Score threshold: 60

This queues 5 × 3 = 15 scrape jobs. Each job imports up to 10 leads, auto-scored 0–100.

Lead Scoring (0–100)

  • Rating — 4.5★+ = 30pts, 4.0★+ = 20pts, 3.5★+ = 10pts
  • Reviews — 100+ = 25pts, 50+ = 15pts, 10+ = 8pts
  • Website — has website = 20pts
  • Email — has email = 15pts
  • Phone — has phone = 10pts

Run Score All Leads at any time to re-score and sort your lead list. Focus outreach on leads scoring 70+.

ICP Builder (Killer Feature #2)

Define your Ideal Customer Profile once and OutreachPro scrapes automatically on a weekly schedule. Go to Settings → ICP Builder, set your cities, search terms, minimum rating, and minimum reviews. Every Monday morning, new matching leads land in your database automatically — no manual scraping needed.

Best Practices

  • Keep searches specific: "E-Bike Händler Köln" beats "Fahrrad"
  • Set minRating to 4.0+ to filter out low-quality businesses
  • Set minReviews to 10+ to ensure the business is active
  • Export leads as CSV after scoring to backup your best prospects
