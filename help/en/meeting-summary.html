Meeting Summary & Follow-Up Drafter

Paste your meeting notes after any call or demo — Claude writes a structured summary, extracts next steps and objections, assesses deal health, and drafts a personalised follow-up email ready to send. Salesforce AI includes meeting summaries at $350+/user/month.

Using It After a Meeting

  1. Go to Lead → Log Activity → Meeting Notes
  2. Paste your raw notes — bullet points, messy sentences, keywords all work
  3. Select language for the follow-up email
  4. Click Summarise
  5. Review the AI-generated summary, next steps, and follow-up email
  6. Copy the follow-up email → Gmail → send

What Claude Extracts

  • Summary — 2-sentence plain-English recap of the meeting
  • Key points — the most important things discussed
  • Next steps — specific actions agreed on, auto-queued in Memory CRM
  • Objections — concerns raised that need addressing in follow-up
  • Sentiment — positive / neutral / negative read on the conversation
  • Deal health — hot / warm / cold / dead assessment
  • Follow-up email — full draft referencing the discussion, with CTA
  • Proposal recommended? — Claude flags if it's time to send a proposal

Example

Raw notes: "Spoke with Max. Very interested. Budget ready Q2.
           Mentioned they use a competitor but not happy with
           support. Wants to see our Pro model. Call back 1 May."

Summary: "Max at Fahrrad Müller is interested and has Q2 budget
          approved, but is currently using a competitor they're
          dissatisfied with."
Next steps: ["Send Pro Platinum brochure", "Follow-up call May 1"]
Objection: Competitor (dissatisfied — opening exists)
Deal health: Hot
Proposal recommended: true
Memory CRM: Follow-up queued May 1

API

POST /api/meetings/summarise
  Body: { leadId, notes, duration, language }
  → { summary, keyPoints[], nextSteps[], objections[],
      sentiment, dealHealth, followUpEmail, proposalRecommended }
