Campaign Management

Each campaign has a name, daily send limit, and sequence steps. Click + New Campaign, set a name and daily limit (50–100 recommended). Go to Sequences to build email steps with delays.

A/B Testing

Enable A/B on any step to split-test two subject lines 50/50.

Status

  • Draft — not launched
  • Active — sending
  • Paused — stopped
  • Completed — done
