Campaigns

A campaign is a container for a sequence of emails sent to a list of leads. OutreachPro handles scheduling, daily limits, stop-on-reply, and A/B subject testing automatically.

Creating a Campaign

  1. Go to Campaigns → New Campaign
  2. Set a name, sender email, and daily send limit (recommended: 20–40/day per inbox)
  3. Toggle Stop on Reply — when a lead replies, OutreachPro stops sending further steps to them automatically
  4. Add leads from your Lead Finder (filter by status: new)
  5. Attach a sequence of email steps (see Sequences)
  6. Click Activate to start sending

A/B Subject Testing (Killer Feature #3)

Test two subject lines against each other automatically. In any campaign, click A/B Test and enter two subject variants:

Subject A: "Quick question about {{name}}'s bikes"
Subject B: "Saw your {{rating}}★ reviews — impressive"

Set the split % (default: 50/50) and auto-promote threshold (default: 50 sends). After 50 sends, OutreachPro calculates which subject got more opens and automatically promotes the winner for all remaining sends. The losing subject is retired.

Campaign Statuses

  • Draft — not sending yet, still being configured
  • Active — sending daily up to the limit
  • Paused — temporarily stopped (resumes from current step)
  • Complete — all leads have received all steps

Daily Limits

Never exceed 50 emails/day per inbox for cold outreach. OutreachPro enforces your daily limit automatically — if you set 30/day, it sends 30 at random intervals throughout the day to avoid spam filters.

LinkedIn Steps

Add LinkedIn connection requests as steps in any sequence (Killer Feature #7). After connecting, follow-up messages can be added as later steps. Claude generates personalised connection notes based on the lead's business profile.
