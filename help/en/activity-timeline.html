Activity Timeline

Every interaction with a lead — email sent, opened, clicked, replied, call logged, meeting booked, LinkedIn message sent — is automatically recorded on a unified timeline per lead. No more hunting across Unibox, Campaigns, and CRM to understand a lead's history. One lead, one timeline, every touchpoint in order.

What Gets Logged Automatically

  • email_sent — every campaign email dispatched
  • email_opened — pixel tracking fires (see Buying Signals)
  • email_clicked — link click recorded
  • replied — Unibox reply received
  • meeting_booked — scheduler slot confirmed
  • linkedin_sent — LinkedIn step executed
  • signal_detected — buying signal triggered
  • workflow_action — automation fired

Logging a Call or Meeting

  1. Open any lead in Lead Finder
  2. Click Log Activity
  3. Select type: Call, Meeting, Note
  4. Write your notes — e.g. "Spoke with Max, interested but wants Q3 budget approval. Said to call back October."
  5. Click Save

Claude extracts: summary, next steps, sentiment, and — if a date is mentioned — automatically creates a Memory CRM follow-up reminder for that date.

API

GET  /api/leads/:id/timeline    → full timeline for one lead
POST /api/activities/log        → {leadId, type, notes, subject, duration}
GET  /api/activities/recent     → last 50 activities across all leads
