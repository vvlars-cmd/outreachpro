Inbox Placement Tester

Test your email subject and body against spam filters before sending a campaign.

How It Works

Enter your subject line and email body snippet, then click Run Placement Test. The system analyses:

  • Spam trigger words in subject and body
  • SPF / DKIM / DMARC DNS record validity
  • Text-to-HTML ratio (too much HTML = spam risk)
  • Presence of an unsubscribe link
  • URL shorteners (avoid bit.ly etc.)

Score Interpretation

  • 80–100 — Low risk, safe to send
  • 60–79 — Medium risk, review warnings
  • Below 60 — High risk, do not send until issues are fixed

Common Fixes

Remove words like "free", "guarantee", "buy now", "click here". Add an unsubscribe link. Configure DMARC record for your domain. Keep image-to-text ratio below 40%.
