Inbox Placement Tester

The Inbox Tester checks whether your emails will land in the primary inbox, promotions tab, or spam folder — before you send a campaign.

Domain Health Dashboard (Killer Feature #1)

The most important deliverability check. Enter your sending domain and OutreachPro checks:

  • SPF — authorises your server to send on behalf of the domain
  • DKIM — cryptographically signs your emails to prove authenticity
  • DMARC — tells email providers what to do with emails that fail SPF/DKIM
  • MX Records — your domain can receive replies
  • Spamhaus Blacklist — your domain isn't on the global spam blocklist

Each check is scored: 5 passes = 100/100 Grade A. Fix any failing checks before sending campaigns.

Setting Up SPF

Add this TXT record to your domain's DNS:

v=spf1 include:_spf.google.com ~all

(Adjust for your email provider — Fastmail, Outlook, custom SMTP, etc.)

Setting Up DMARC

Add this TXT record at _dmarc.yourdomain.com:

v=DMARC1; p=none; rua=mailto:dmarc@yourdomain.com

Start with p=none (monitoring only), then move to p=quarantine after 2 weeks of clean reports.

What to Do If You Fail Checks

  • No SPF — add the TXT record above to your domain DNS (propagates in 24h)
  • No DKIM — enable DKIM signing in your email provider (Google Workspace: Admin → Apps → Gmail → Authenticate Email)
  • No DMARC — add the TXT record above
  • Blacklisted — request delisting at mxtoolbox.com/blacklists. Takes 24–48h.
