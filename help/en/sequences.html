Sequence Builder

Sequences are the step-by-step email flows that run inside a campaign. Each campaign can have 3–7 steps with configurable delays between them.

Building a Sequence

  1. Go to Sequences and select a campaign from the dropdown
  2. Click + Add Step to add an email step
  3. Click the step to edit its subject, body, and send delay
  4. Click Save Sequence to persist

Delay Configuration

Delay is measured in days from campaign start. Recommended pattern: Step 1 on Day 1, Step 2 on Day 4, Step 3 on Day 10, Step 4 on Day 20. This gives leads time to see each email before the follow-up.

A/B Testing

Enable A/B on any step to create two subject line variants. The system automatically splits leads 50/50 and tracks which variant generates more opens and replies. After 50+ sends, the winning variant is identified in Analytics.

Stop on Reply

When "Stop on reply" is enabled on the campaign, a lead is automatically removed from the sequence the moment they reply to any step. This prevents over-emailing prospects who have already engaged.
