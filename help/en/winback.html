Win-Back & Re-Engagement Campaigns

Leads who went cold 90+ days ago, or deals that were marked Lost, get a completely fresh outreach — not a follow-up, but a genuine win-back email that acknowledges time has passed and circumstances may have changed. These convert at 15–25% because the lead already knows you.

Finding Win-Back Candidates

  1. Go to Campaigns → Win-Back
  2. Set the cutoff (default: 90 days)
  3. OutreachPro shows: Lost deals older than cutoff + cold leads with no activity in that period
  4. Review the list — remove any who explicitly said never
  5. Click Generate Emails for selected leads

What Makes a Good Win-Back Email

Claude writes these with a specific structure:

  • Acknowledge time has passed — "It's been a while since we last spoke"
  • Reference why they didn't buy — timing, budget, competitor — without being pushy
  • Offer something new — updated product, new case study, different pricing
  • Low-pressure CTA — "No obligation, just wondering if anything has changed"

Example

Subject: "Es ist eine Weile her — Fahrrad Müller"

Hallo,

ich melde mich kurz nach einigen Monaten. Damals kam das
Timing nicht ganz hin — ich verstehe das vollkommen.

Seitdem haben wir einige Verbesserungen am CycleWASH Pro
vorgenommen, die besonders für Betriebe Ihrer Größe
interessant sein könnten.

Ist das Thema heute vielleicht wieder relevant?

Keine Verpflichtung — ich bin einfach neugierig.

Beste Grüße

Best Timing

  • 90 days after going cold — first win-back attempt
  • 6 months — second attempt with a different angle
  • 12 months — final attempt, reference the year anniversary

API

GET  /api/winback/candidates?daysOld=90  → list of lost deals + cold leads
POST /api/winback/generate               → {leadId, reason, language} → {email}
