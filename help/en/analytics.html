Analytics Dashboard

Track outreach performance across all campaigns, with per-campaign and aggregate breakdowns.

Key Metrics

  • Open rate — percentage of sent emails opened (industry average: 20–30%)
  • Reply rate — percentage that generated a reply (good: 5–10%, excellent: 10%+)
  • Bounce rate — invalid addresses (keep below 2%)
  • Pipeline value — qualified leads × product price

Time Ranges

Use the dropdown to switch between 7-day, 30-day, and 90-day views. The bar chart shows weekly send/reply trends. Use this to spot which days generate the best replies.

Campaign Breakdown

The bottom table shows per-campaign performance. Campaigns with reply rates below 3% should be paused and the copy reviewed. Try a different subject line, shorter body, or more personalised opening.

Pipeline to Revenue

Qualified leads in CRM automatically contribute to Pipeline Value. When a deal is marked Won in CRM, it moves to Won Revenue. Track the conversion: Leads → Contacted → Qualified → Won.
