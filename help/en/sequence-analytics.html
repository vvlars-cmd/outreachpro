Sequence Step Analytics

See exactly which step of every Smart Sequence gets the best open rates, click rates, and reply rates. Find the drop-off point where leads stop engaging — and fix it. Most tools don't show step-level data. Salesforce includes this at $175+/user/month.

What Gets Tracked Per Step

  • Sent — how many leads received this step
  • Opened — pixel tracking fires, recorded as an open event
  • Clicked — lead clicked a link in this step
  • Replied — lead replied at this step
  • Open rate — opened ÷ sent × 100
  • Reply rate — replied ÷ sent × 100 — the most important metric

Reading the Data

  • High open, low reply on Step 1 — subject line works, but body copy or CTA is weak. Rewrite the ask.
  • Step 2 open rate drops sharply vs Step 1 — too long a delay, or subject line for Step 2 is poor.
  • Step 3 has the highest reply rate — common. Third touch gets replies. Make sure you're sending Step 3.
  • LinkedIn step has high conversion — multichannel is working. Add LinkedIn to other sequences.

Using This to Optimise

  1. Go to Analytics → Sequence Performance
  2. Sort by reply rate — which step is your best performer?
  3. The "Best Step" field shows the single highest-converting step in each sequence
  4. Clone that step's approach into Step 1 of your next sequence

API

GET /api/analytics/sequences
→ { sequences: [{ name, enrolled, converted, conversionRate,
    stepBreakdown: [{ stepName, sent, openRate, replyRate }],
    bestStep }] }
