Email Warmup

Warmup gradually increases your sending volume to build sender reputation with Gmail, Outlook, and other mail providers. New domains need warmup before high-volume sending.

How the Ramp Works

OutreachPro starts at 5 emails/day and increases by 5 each week until reaching your configured daily target. Week 1: 5/day → Week 2: 10/day → Week 6: 30/day.

Deliverability Score

The score (0–100) reflects your inbox placement rate across a network of 2,800+ warmup peers. A score above 80 means you're landing in the inbox reliably. Below 60 means spam risk.

SPF / DKIM / DMARC

Before warming up, ensure your domain has all three DNS records configured. Use the Inbox Tester to verify. Missing DMARC is the most common cause of spam folder placement.

Best Practices

  • Never send more than 50 emails/day from a new domain in the first month
  • Keep bounce rate below 2%
  • Maintain reply rate above 5% — replies signal positive engagement to mail providers
  • Avoid spam trigger words in subject lines (use the Inbox Tester to check)
