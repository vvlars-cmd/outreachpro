Intent Signal Detector

The Intent Signal Detector monitors your leads' Google Business profiles for changes that indicate growth or buying intent — then flags the hottest prospects for immediate outreach.

What Counts as a Signal

  • Review surge — 5+ new reviews since last scan = business is active and growing
  • Rating improvement — rating went up = business is improving, good time to reach out
  • New photos — business is actively managing their profile (future enhancement)

Running a Scan

  1. Go to Analytics → Intent Signals
  2. Click Scan Leads (scans up to 20 leads per run)
  3. Set limit if needed (default: 20)
  4. Results appear showing which leads triggered signals and their signal score

Signal Scoring

  • Review growth (+5 reviews): +40 points
  • Rating improvement: +30 points
  • Combined: 70+ points = hot lead 🔥

Taking Action

Click Action on any signal to mark it handled. This moves it from the active signals list to history. Best practice: action a signal by sending the lead a personalised email within 24 hours of detection — timing is everything.

Why This Matters

A business with rapidly growing reviews is investing in their reputation. They're likely growing their revenue and open to tools that help them scale. Reaching out at this exact moment — when they're actively improving — converts at 3–5× the rate of cold outreach.

API

GET  /api/intent-signals              → list all signals
POST /api/intent-signals/scan         → run scan {limit: 20}
POST /api/intent-signals/:id/action   → mark actioned
